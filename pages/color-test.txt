<p>Making a bare-bones HSL color picker with Mavo expressions:</p>

<div style="background-color: [color]">
	hue : <input type="range" property="hue" max="360" /><br />
	saturation : <input type="range" property="saturation" /><br />
	lightness : <input type="range" property="lightness" />
<br />

	<input property="color" value="hsl([hue], [saturation]%, [lightness]%)" readonly />
</div>
